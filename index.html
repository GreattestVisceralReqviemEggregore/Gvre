About this clan

More than a collective, GVRE is a shared frequency for those who resonate with the discipline of progress. Our structure welcomes both the virtuoso who defies the impossible and the enthusiast who finds delight in daily improvement. We open our shadows to those seeking a refuge of common interests, where dialogue and play intertwine in the pursuit of a higher purpose. (Our focus is solely on 4k).

Rules and Conditions

Rule I — Respect

All players within clan spaces must be very respectful of each other; we have 0% tolerance for any disrespect.

Rule II — Fair Play

Every play, especially those submitted for publication on our social media, must be 100% legitimate. If cheating is detected, the replay will be disqualified.

Rule III — Common Sense

If there is something that is not in these three simple rules and is not good for the other members or for the image of the clan, it is best to avoid it.

Condition I

Each replay must be strictly submitted via Discord or a download link for the video must be sent via DM on TikTok.

Condition II

The Lvst Dans replays need to be verified; you can use a handcam or a key overlay.

Condition III

For replays to be accepted, the username must include the tag [GVRE].

Condition IV

For the clan leaderboards, the following will be evaluated: PP, Dans, Lvst Dans and current performance.
